## Code of Conduct

### Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of this project, pledge to make participation in TalkSpace a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, ethnicity, religion, nationality, or level of experience.

---

### Our Standards

We expect all participants to contribute to a positive environment. Examples of acceptable behavior include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language  
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences  
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully  
- Focusing on what is best for the community  
- Showing empathy towards other community members  

---

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind  
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments  
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without explicit permission  
- Personal or political attacks  
- Any conduct that is unprofessional or harmful to the community  

---

### Responsibilities of Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

---

### Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including but not limited to:
- GitHub repositories
- Issues and pull requests
- Discussions and comments
- Any other community interaction related to this project

---

### Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer.  
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

---

### Commitment

By participating in this project, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct and help create a safe and positive environment for everyone.
